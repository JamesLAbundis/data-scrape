Democratic National Analysis

Below is an in-depth analysis of the Democratic Primary up until late March. 28 states are included in total. The analysis includes determining what factors such as race, sex, income, etc., contribute to the fraction of the vote each candidate wins. A multi-variable linear regression model was used to test these factors and predict future primaries.
