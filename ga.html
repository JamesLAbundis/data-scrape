High School Graduate or Higher

The graph between the fraction of votes and the percentage of people who graduated from high school shows a positive correlation for Bernie Sanders and Ted Cruz. On the other hand, there is a negative correlation for Hillary Clinton and Donald Trump.

Percentage of Whites not Including Hispanic/Latino

The graph between the fraction of votes and the percentage of white people shows a positive correlation for Bernie Sanders and Ted Cruz. On the other hand, there is a negative correlation for Hillary Clinton. Looking at the data points for Donald Trump suggest and insignficant correlation between these two variables.

People 65 Years or Older

The graph between the fraction of votes and the percentage of people over 65 years of age shows a negative correlation for Bernie Sanders and Ted Cruz. On the other hand, there is a positive correlation for Hillary Clinton and Donald Trump.
