Summary Analysis

Bernie tends to be more popular in counties with high average income and high high school graduation rates, whereas Hillary is the opposite: She tends to be more popular amongst counties with lower average income and lower high school graduate rates. Interestingly enough, Trump tends to follow the same correlation patterns as Hillary in many of the plots.
